Course Description

The AVA Academy Executive Assistant Certification Program is a rigorous, hands-on training designed to prepare high-performing professionals for modern, remote executive support roles. In just 4–6 weeks, students gain essential skills to become indispensable to busy entrepreneurs, CEOs, and online business owners.
This self-paced program, paired with weekly mentor guidance, covers everything from calendar and inbox management to task delegation, SOP development, and client communication. You'll learn to build daily check-in systems, organize executive workflows, and confidently manage digital tools that top businesses use every day. The curriculum also includes specialized training in AI tools like ChatGPT for task automation and productivity enhancement.
What sets AVA apart is its focus on real-world readiness. The final project involves building a complete Executive Assistant SOP for a real business, giving students tangible experience and portfolio-ready work. Graduates emerge not only certified but confident—with the mindset, systems, and leadership habits needed to thrive in fast-paced business environments
Whether you're pivoting careers or leveling up your operations career path, this certification unlocks doors to remote, high-impact roles across industries. Upon completion, you’ll join a growing network of certified EAs and gain early access to exclusive job opportunities.

What You’ll Learn from AVA Academy

  • Master Task List Development
  • Mentored by active executives
  • Daily Check-Ins & EOD Reporting
  • Client & Team Communication Protocols
  • ChatGPT Prompt Engineering for EA Tasks
  • Final SOP Project Based on a Real Business
