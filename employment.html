Founder Story

AVA Academy was born out
of a real frustration.

AVA Academy was founded by entrepreneurs who struggled to find
Virtual Assistants truly ready for high-level support. Most VA
programs lacked leadership training, real-world business systems,
and critical thinking.

So, we created what we couldn’t find:

A rigorous, mentorship-driven program that turns capable
individuals into world-class Executive Assistants — ready to lead,
organize, and make an impact from Day One.
