About AVA Academy

AVA Academy is the gold standard in Virtual Executive Assistant education. Founded by entrepreneurs who understand what business owners really need, we offer a selective, rigorous program that produces EAs who are:

  • Trained in real-world business operations
  • Certified in communication, organization, and leadership
  • Mentored by active executives
  • Evaluated through hands-on final exams for real businesses

Faq

How AVA Academy Works Explained

How long is the program?

The program duration varies based on the specific track and individual progress. Typically, it ranges from 6 to 12 months for completion.

What does the program cost?

A: We offer both self-paid and sponsored tracks. Scholarships available
for top applicants.

Can I hire a trainee before they graduate?

Yes, we encourage early engagement. Companies can connect with trainees for internships or employment opportunities before graduation, based on their performance and readiness.

What makes your certification different?

Our certification is industry-recognized, project-based, and includes hands-on experience with real-world scenarios, ensuring graduates are job-ready.
